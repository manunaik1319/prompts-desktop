What is Promptly?

Promptly is a powerful browser extension designed to help you communicate more effectively with AI. It transforms your rough ideas into clear, structured, and professional prompts.

  • Works with ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and more
  • Multiple tone options for different contexts
  • One-click improvement with instant results
  • Privacy-focused: your data stays private

Meet the Founder

Built with a simple goal: to make AI more accessible and useful for everyone. Our founder is passionate about productivity, clean design, and helping people unlock the full potential of AI tools.
